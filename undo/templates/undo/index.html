Reddit Account Deleter

Reddit is cool. But there are times when you need to nuke your entire presence on the website. Deleting everything you've ever posted to the sites. Comments, self-posts and link-posts, the whole package.

The process is pretty simple. First go authenticate this site to make the changes on your behalf. Then a process will be started to nuke your account. It will take a little over 2 seconds for each comment and submission you've ever made. So if you've made a bunch, then it might take a while.

After that, all that's left is deleting the account itself. This step you'll need to take yourself by going to http://www.reddit.com/prefs/delete/ and clicking the delete button. Your account will now have been completely nuked.
